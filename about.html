About Me

I am Dualo, also known as Dee Jay Domoguen, currently taking a Bachelor of Science in Criminology. My passions include photography, drawing, motorcycle riding, hiking, and hitting the gym in my free time.

I began my education at Ambagiw Elementary School, then pursued junior high school at Agawa National High School. For senior high, I took the STEM strand at St. James High School of Mt. Province Inc.. There, I had the opportunity to work alongside BFP officers during our work immersion program.

I gained hands-on knowledge in Basic Life Support, knot tying, firefighting, and rappelling. I also participated in the BLS competition during the Ubaya Festival, where our team was awarded for Best in Knot Tying and Fastest Time to Finish the Course.

My journey reflects my love for both the creative and the physical, always striving for growth, discipline, and purpose.
